VirtualEnv & Data Visualization

Action: Studied virtualenv & altair

Need to learn how to create virtualenv on windows machine.

Referenced this site for installing & activating virtualenv on windows:

https://programwithus.com/learn-to-code/Pip-and-virtualenv-on-Windows/

Once you are in env\Scripts, you can run .\activate (via Powershell) and you will have the following indication that you are in your virtual environment:

PS C:\py\lottery\env\Scripts> .\activate (env) PS C:\py\lottery\env\Scripts>
